Book name: In the Forest

Author: John Masson

Publish: 2022

Description: Something sinister is brewing deep within a pitch-black, thick forest. Upon deciding to go camping in the forest, a group of friends quickly learn that they are not alone. Their every move is plagued by strange noises, enigmatic figures, and horrific images, and it soon becomes apparent that something lurking in the trees wants to kill them. The trio starts to solve a deadly mystery that has been buried in the forest for ages as they try to survive against the unrelenting onslaught of horror. The suspenseful, terrifying, and spine-tingling horror book "In the Forest" is sure to keep you up all night long, terrified to turn out the lights.
