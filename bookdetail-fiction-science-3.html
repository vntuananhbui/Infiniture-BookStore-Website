Book name: Dream of a Distant Home

Author: Aaron Loeb

Publish: 2021

Description: "Dream of a Distant Home" is a book that chronicles the experience of an astronaut coming back to Earth after a mission in space. In addition to describing the meticulous planning and execution by mission controllers and support teams, the book also describes the physical and psychological difficulties the astronaut endures throughout the crucial portion of the trip. The book provides a glimpse into the exhilarating and mind-blowing adventure of returning home through vivid descriptions and personal anecdotes, stressing the talent, bravery, and dedication of the astronaut and the teams who make it possible. Readers are taken on an extraordinary voyage through space and back to Earth in "Dream of a Distant Home" a gripping read.
