Book name: History of Civilization

Author: Tom Jamester

Publish: 2020

Description: From the beginning of early civilizations to the present, "The History of Civilization" provides a thorough analysis of human societies and cultures. The book discusses many different subjects, such as the rise and fall of empires, scientific and technological developments, artistic and cultural movements, and social and political revolutions. It gives readers a thorough grasp of the significant occurrences and advancements that have shaped human civilisation, from the prehistoric civilizations of Mesopotamia and Egypt to the contemporary, globally connected world. Additionally, "The History of Civilization" emphasizes the variety and complexity of historical human communities and cultures as well as how these interactions have shaped the development of civilization as a whole.
