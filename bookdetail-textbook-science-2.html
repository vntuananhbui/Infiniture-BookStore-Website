Book name: USB Technology

Author: Jonathan Patterson

Publish: 2022

Description: "USB Technology" is a thorough reference to the development, application, and usage of USB (Universal Serial Bus) technology, a standard interface for attaching gadgets to computers and other electronic devices. The history and development of USB, the physical and electrical specifications of USB connectors and cables, USB device classes and protocols, USB software architecture, and USB security issues are just a few of the many subjects covered in the book. It gives readers a comprehensive overview of the technological specifics and real-world factors involved in creating and implementing USB-based systems and devices. Case studies, real-world scenarios, examples, and helpful suggestions are also included in the book to aid readers in applying the topics discussed.
