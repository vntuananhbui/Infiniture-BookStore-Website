Book name: Build Your Self

Author: Olivia Watson

Publish: 2020

Description: A self-improvement book called "Build Your Self" focuses on personal growth and development. Self-awareness, self-reflection, and self-discovery are all covered in the book. Additionally, the author offers helpful advice on how to cultivate a positive self-perception, increase confidence, and get rid of limiting beliefs. The book also offers activities and tips for creating and accomplishing personal objectives, forming wholesome routines, and creating wholesome connections. In general, "Build Your Self" is a motivating tool for everyone looking to better themselves and lead a more contented life.
